## Foundation
- Dense red meats
    - Cow, sheep, camel
- Nightshades
  - Potatoes, tomatoes, tobacco
- Full fat milk, heavy cream
- Nuts and yogurt
- Fatty pelagic fish
- Grains
  - White rice, fermented wheat


## Incredible Vitality
- Hearts
- Livers
- Brains
- Bone marrow
- Raw onion and garlic 
- Pine pollen
- Raw ginseng
